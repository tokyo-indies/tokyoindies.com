January Summary

2017-03-14T00:40:53.650+0900

Happy New Year Indies! Here's what happened this month:

The Global Game Jam is taking place January 20-22; there's still time to get in if you're interested.

Hawken King presented a game made for the Nintendo Disk System!! He created a cartridge based on the soundtrack for his recently released game, Pirate Pop Plus.

Martin from Ninja Egg presented Kyub, their action puzzle game available on XBox One and Windows. Using the power of elements and your brain you have to guide a cube through a series of bizarre and beautiful environments.

Ryosuke Mihara talked about the Pico-8 Tiny TV Jam, in progress until the 22nd.

That was all this month, catch you next time!
